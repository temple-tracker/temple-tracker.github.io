Community Guidelines

We request every user to use Temple Tracker respectfully and responsibly.

Temple Tracker is a spiritual and community-focused platform. Please help us keep it useful and peaceful for everyone.

check_circle Allowed Content

You may use Temple Tracker to:

  • Add temple details
  • Share pooja or festival information
  • Post genuine temple-related alerts
  • Inform devotees about spiritual gatherings or community events

cancel Not Allowed

Please do NOT:

  • Post fake or misleading information
  • Upload offensive or abusive content
  • Hurt religious sentiments
  • Use political, caste-based, or hateful language
  • Post personal attacks against priests, trustees, or devotees
  • Share unverified corruption or legal allegations
  • Use Temple Tracker for advertising unrelated businesses
  • Upload copyrighted or inappropriate content

security Temple Verification

All newly added temples are manually reviewed by our team before approval.

We may edit, reject, or remove any information that appears incorrect, offensive, misleading, or unrelated to the purpose of the platform.

warning Alerts & Notices

Please post only genuine notices that are useful for devotees.

False emergency messages, rumors, or sensitive content may result in content removal or account restrictions.

self_improvement Respect for All Temples

Temple Tracker respects all temples, traditions, and spiritual practices.

Users are expected to maintain mutual respect while using the platform.

flag Reporting Content

If you find incorrect or inappropriate content, please use the report option or contact us directly.

info Disclaimer

Temple Tracker is a community-driven informational platform. Information shared by users may be subject to verification and should not be treated as official government or legal records.
