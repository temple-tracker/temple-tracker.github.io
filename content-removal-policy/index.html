Content Removal Policy

Our commitment to a safe, respectful, and useful platform.

Temple Tracker respects all temples, traditions, devotees, and communities. We are committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and useful platform for everyone.

delete_sweep Reasons for Content Removal

Content may be removed from Temple Tracker if it:

  • Contains false or misleading information
  • Hurts religious sentiments
  • Includes abusive, hateful, or offensive language
  • Contains political propaganda or unrelated promotional material
  • Violates someone’s privacy
  • Includes unverified accusations or defamatory content
  • Contains inappropriate images or media
  • Violates copyright or intellectual property rights
  • Appears to be spam, fake activity, or misuse of the platform
pending_actions

We may also temporarily remove content while it is under manual review.

report_problem Reporting Content

Users can report any content that appears:

  • Incorrect
  • Offensive
  • Misleading
  • Fake
  • Inappropriate

Reported content will be reviewed by our moderation team.

verified Temple Verification

New temple entries submitted by users are manually verified before becoming publicly visible on the platform.

Temple Tracker reserves the right to approve, reject, edit, or remove any submission based on platform guidelines and verification status.

gavel Account Restrictions

Users repeatedly posting misleading, abusive, or harmful content may face:

  • Temporary restrictions
  • Permanent account suspension
  • Removal of submitted content

contact_support Contact

If you believe your content was removed incorrectly or if you want to request content removal, you may contact the Temple Tracker support team through the contact section available on the platform.
