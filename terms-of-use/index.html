Terms of Use — Temple Tracker

1. Purpose of the Application

Temple Tracker is a community-driven, free-to-use platform to:

  • Share temple events and pooja timings.
  • Organize spiritual gatherings.
  • Record temple history and prevent land or idol misuse.

2. User Responsibilities

  • Share accurate and respectful information.
  • Do not post offensive, false, or harmful content.
  • Respect the religious and cultural sentiments of all users.

3. No Commercial Intent

Temple Tracker does not charge any fees from users. This platform exists solely for cultural and community purposes.

4. Content Ownership

Content shared by users remains their own. By posting, you give us permission to display your content publicly within the app.

5. Limitation of Liability

  • We are not responsible for inaccuracies in user-contributed information.
  • Issues arising from community gatherings (like event cancellations or disputes).
  • Any misuse of information by third parties.

6. Account Termination

Temple Tracker reserves the right to remove content or suspend users who violate these terms.
